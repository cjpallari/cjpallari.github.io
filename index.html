Assignment 2

After a shaky start to the year, the San Francisco Giants currently sit at 66-66, which puts them 12.5 games out of the division lead in the National League West. While their hopes of winning the division are essentially gone, they still have a chance to buy a ticket to the dance via the Wild Card. With a surplus of veteran talent (particularly in their starting pitching rotation) they could prove to be a scary team to face in a playoff series.
